About:
Sunder Nursery is in the heart of the city of Delhi. Originally known as Azim Bagh and built by the Mughals in the 16th century, it lies on the Mughal-era Grand Trunk Road. There are a number of attractions in the Sunder Nursery which include the Flower Valley, Garden Amphitheatre, Mughal World Heritage Sites, Peacock Habitat and many more.
