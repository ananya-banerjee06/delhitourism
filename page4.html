Lotus Temple

Did you know? The building is made up of 27 unsupported marble ‘petals’ arranged in the shape of a lotus.

Timings

Closing (summer) 7 pm
Opening (winter) 9 am

About:
Located in the capital city of New Delhi, the Lotus Temple is a beautiful structure established and dedicated to the Baha’i faith. The magnificent structure of the building is created in the form of a stunning white petal lotus flower. One of the most attractive things about the Lotus Temple, other than its structure, is the fact that this temple propagates the oneness of God, the Almighty. Furthermore, it is open to all kinds of people, regardless of their religion, gender, race or even nationality.
